Itophiyaho Gobboomu Deerinni Massagannote Bissara Dhukka Gaamarsate 10(Tonnu Barrira Mootimma addi addi Qoqqowu Quchumara Qajeelishsha uyiittinno. Sidaamu qoqqowinninno woradate,quchumu gashshootinni,zoonete tantannonni hattonno qoqqowu mereershshinni hadhinnori massaganno, dagatenna gobbate ikkitanno owaante aate qajeelishsha gudde seeda doogonni keerunni higginno. Sidaamu qoqqowi eote biilloonyi loossassinne mannajimentete miilla ledo,haaro hajuujanna woyabbinno owaante aate hexxonni higginnore biilloonyu massagaanno ayirinye,baxxille,danchumanna haggiire xawisanno amanyootinni,hawalle keerunni daginni yaatenni haggire xawisinno.
